How Company Works#

When you create an account we also create company profile linked to your account. The company profile is that profile for which your OAuth is connected to. You will usually never interact with company profile which why you edit the company details execpt the mailing address. Each company is linked to one Square account and allowed only 1 OAL app. If you have multiple brands under one company, create a new account for each brand.
