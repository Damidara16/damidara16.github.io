How to manage email invites

To manage your email invites, go to the invite management page from the apps tab on the nav bar. Here you can edit the emails and names of the people you have on your invite list, then click save changes in the top right once you are finished. The invite list is linked to your company and is used to determine which people in your organization should receive your OAL app to verify and test it before it goes live. You have a maximum of 5 invites, you could either use for different people or different devices. Instructions on how to download the app will be included their invite emails.
