    Create a category

    Steps#

    • Navigate
    • Provide name
    • Select Category
    • Select Icon

    To create a category, navigate to the menus tab on the sidebar and click categories, there you can view all existing categories.

    1. Click the "add category" button, a module will appear prompting you to create a category.

    1. Provide a given for the category, this name does not have to be name of the category in your Square. The name provided will be the name shown to your customer on your OAL app.

    1. Click on the Select Square Category dropdown, there you'll see a list of all your categories from your Square account. Making it easier for create categories and have data related to orders and categories sync to your Square.

    1. When your ready to create your category, click create category. From there you'll be directed to choose an icon for your category. There are multiple category families that you are able to choose from so that you are able to find one that fits your brand the most. The icon you select will be shown to your customer's in your OAL app and can be changed at anytime. We're also always adding more icons. Its best to choose icons from same family for a menu, so that your app has cohesion and looks coherent, however your free to choose as you please.

    1. To select a icon, click on the icon and a confirmation module will appear. Confirm your selection to set the icon for the category.
